About me

A child's brain is built, not born. I have been keen to observe the trajectory of learning: my own and that of others. To learn from research reports what is ailing pedagogies and what achievable actions are appropriate for each challenge. Ranging from misaligned components of education systems down to slower ineffective systems. I have been struck by how proposed solutions have failed and why they failed. I have alighted from that ride, at a passion for creating fundamental learning foundations in young children. I seek to triangulate the school and social environment aspects of learning to pursue better learning outcomes. Seeing that rural communities have disproportionately bigger gaps, that is where my laser of efforts will try to pierce. In that light I have founded BEAM. I guess we'll be patient to see how it rolls out.
